md2pptx

Browser-based Markdown to PowerPoint converter. Write in the editor or load a .md file (drag and drop supported). Split slides with --- or #/## headings. Bullets (-), numbered lists (1.), bold (**text**), italic (*text*), and fenced code blocks are supported. Blockquotes and ### Notes: sections become speaker notes. The preview updates live, tracks your cursor, and reflects the selected PPTX colour scheme (Theme, Light, Dark, Minimal). The output filename auto-fills from the first slide title. Press Cmd/Ctrl+Enter to convert. No installation, no server, no account.
